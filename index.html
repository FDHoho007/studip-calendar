StudIP Calendar

This mobile app allows you to share your StudIP calendar with your friends and view all of your calendars in one place, so you can arrange lunch or other gatherings in the most efficient way.

To get started, please choose a display name that is shown to your friends as well as a color for events of your personal StudIP calendar.

Next up you will need your personal StudIP calendar ics url. You can retrieve it from studip.uni-passau.de/studip/dispatch.php/calendar/single/share
